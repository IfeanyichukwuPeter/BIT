About Us

BIT Children Ministry is a Bible-based ministry focused on guiding children/teenagers in their spiritual growth and character development. We provide a safe and structured environment where young people are taught, mentored, and cared for with intention.

Who We Are

We are ministry committed to helping teenagers grow in their understanding of God's Word and in the way they live their daily lives. Through teaching and mentorship, and well-planned programs, we work alongside parents and guardians to support the spiritual and personal growth of every child.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

  • To raise a generation that knows Christ, lives with purpose and reflect God's love in everyday life
  • To encourage a life of prayer, obedience, and faith in the life of the children/teennagers.

Our Vision

To raise a genration of children/teenagers who know God, are rooted in biblical values, and live out their faith with purpose — impacting their homes, schools, and society with truth, love, and integrity.

What We Do

  • Provide guidance and support through dedicated brothers' and sisters' coordinators
  • Run structured programs that support growth and mentorship
  • Organize events and activities that encourage learning, discipline, and fellowship
